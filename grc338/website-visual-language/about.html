About us

The Soup Stop began as a humble booth at the local farmer’s market, selling matzoh ball soup under the name “Mama’s Matzoh”.

The Matzoh Ball soup quickly became a staple at the farmers market, selling multiple types of soup and customers lining up down the block for her delicious and unique soup creations. Encouraged by the booming success, we changed our name to the Soup Stop and opened up a storefront in town.

Our restaurant quickly became known for its quirky and eclectic decor, including vintage soup tureens and quirky artwork inspired by Andy Warhol. Customers loved the cozy and welcoming atmosphere, spreading the word and increasing our popularity exponentially.

At our restaurant, soup is the main event. We believe that a good bowl of soup can be comforting, nourishing, and a perfect dish no matter the weather. In addition to our soup menu, we also offer a selection of sides to round out your meal. And for those who are looking for a quick and convenient option, we offer takeout and delivery services, so you can enjoy our delicious soups wherever you are.

We offer a wide variety of soup options, catering to all dietary needs and tastes- including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and dairy-free options. Whether you're in the mood for something hearty and filling, light and refreshing, or spicy and exotic, we've got you covered. Our soups are made from scratch daily, using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients. We pride ourselves on using locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible, to support our community and offer the best flavors possible. But no matter how much we've grown, we've always stayed true to our roots, offering affordable, high-quality soup dishes that are made with love and care.

Over the years, our soup restaurant has become a beloved fixture in the community, attracting customers from all walks of life. We're proud to be a part of the rich history of our community, and we look forward to serving up delicious soups for many years to come.
